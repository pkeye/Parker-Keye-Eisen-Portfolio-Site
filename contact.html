Contact

The best way to contact me is via email:

eisenparker@gmail.com

If you want me to color, direct, shoot, write, edit, or produce, drop me a line. I want to connect with as many filmmakers as possible, especially Michigan ones. I’m always happy to talk movies, the state of the industry, and ways we can build a community of filmmakers.

I’m currently looking to build my technical writing portfolio and would be happy to assist anyone in creating documentation, manuals, or instruction guides.

This website has been an overview of my work but I also love not working. In my free time I love hanging out with my wife, family, my cats Ingrid and Margot, and watching all Detroit sports (Go Tigers!). Much of my time is also dedicated to reading philosophy, radical political texts, political economy, fiction, and whatever random thing piques my interest. Some of my hobbies outside of film include journaling, note-taking, common-placing, wood working, and computing projects.

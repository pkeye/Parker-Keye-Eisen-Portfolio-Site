Parker Keye Eisen

freelance video editor, colorist, content and technical writer

Since graduating in the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic, I’ve been freelancing as a writer and filmmaker, now under Eisen Media Services LLC. You may look at my resume and think: Why all the different fields, topics, and niches? The answer is simple: I love learning and researching. This quality of mine gives me an incredible set of skills in problem-solving, researching, and critical thinking. Take this website, for example, which I built with HTML and CSS because I wanted to learn to code and was sick of paying massive fees for WYSIWYG platforms.

The long and short of it is that if I want to solve a problem, I learn everything I can and build the skills I don’t already have. That’s why I love filmmaking. It flexes both the technical and creative sides of my brain, at the same time. It’s also why I want to pivot my freelance writing to technical writing and decode complex topics to put them in a form that anyone can read. So, if you’re looking for a writer who is willing to give it all they have for your project, you’ve found one.

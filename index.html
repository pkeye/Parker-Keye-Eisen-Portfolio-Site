Welcome

My name is Parker Keye Eisen and I’m a freelance writer, filmmaker, and colorist. I’m based in Metro Detroit, Michigan. My debut feature film, The Lovers, premiered in 2023 and is now free to stream on 640 Films YouTube, where it has garnered over 86k views. In addition to my work as a director, I’ve been an assistant director and producer on multiple features and shorts. My work explores alienation, exploitation, and interpersonal relationships. For work, I do a lot of things but most often work in content marketing, creating blogs and articles, but I also work as a video editor in a range of fields.

The "film work" page details all of the films I've worked on the various roles I've performed in producing, on-set, and in post. The "color work" section shows stills, my reel, and links (when availble) for all of my work as a colorist. I’d love to collaborate with you! If you’re looking to hire me as a writer, video editor, etc. you’ll want to dive into my Portfolio page where I have a traditional resume, more info about work as a freelance writer, and as many projects as I’m allowed to list. I’ve also listed some personal projects there to encapsulate the things I’m interested in outside of my work as a writer and filmmaker.

I’m currently looking to build my technical writing portfolio and would be happy to assist anyone in creating documentation, manuals, or instruction guides.
